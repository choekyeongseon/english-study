# 문법 (Grammar Notes)

핵심 문법 항목별 정리

---

## must be / must have been (강한 추측)

| 시제 | 구조 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 현재 추측 | must be | You must be really hungry. |
| 과거 추측 | must have been | You must've been really hungry. |

> 조동사(must/should..)와 현재완료가 결합하면 과거 시점에 대한 이야기가 됨

---

## should — 의무/충고/예상

| 용법 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 의무/충고 | ~하는 게 좋겠다 | You should obey your parents. |
| 예상/기대 | 아마 ~일 것이다 | They should be here soon. |

---

## used to — 과거의 습관/상태

| 구조 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| used to + 동사원형 | 과거 습관 | We used to go swimming. |
| be used to + ing | ~하는 데 익숙하다 | She is used to studying at night. |
| get used to + 명사 | ~에 익숙해지다 | They got used to eating Korean food. |

---

## be / get / become

| 동사 | 의미 | 예시 |
|------|------|------|
| be | 상태 (~이다) | We **were** complete strangers. |
| get | 상태의 변화 (~되다) | We **got** really close. |
| become | 변화의 결과 (~가 됐다) | We **have become** best friends. |

---

## bored vs boring (감정 형용사)

| 형태 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| boring | 지루하게 만드는 (원인) | The movie was boring. |
| bored | 지루해진 (결과, 감정) | I'm bored. |
| exciting / excited | 흥미롭게 만드는 / 흥분된 | It's exciting. / I'm excited. |
| confusing / confused | 헷갈리게 만드는 / 헷갈리는 | It's confusing. / I'm confused. |

## 간접화법 (Indirect Speech)

| 직접화법 | 간접화법 |
|----------|---------|
| "I am ~" | she said she **was** ~ |
| "I will ~" | she said she **would** ~ |
| "Can I ~?" | she asked if she **could** ~ |

---

## as ~ as 비교 구문

### Type 1 — 특징/성격 비교
* `My friend is not as tall as me.`
* `I think today is not as cold as yesterday.`

### Type 2 — 정도/빈도 비교
* `I can't eat as quickly as others.`
* `I don't work out as often as her.`

### Type 3 — 양(量) 비교
* `I don't have as much time as you.`

### Type 4 — 다른 시점과의 비교
* `My phone is not as fast as it was when I first got it.`

---

## which — 덧붙이는 관계절

| 두 문장 | which 결합 |
|---------|-----------|
| I got up at 7. It was earlier than usual. | I got up at 7, **which** was earlier than usual. |

> `which`를 해석할 때: "그리고 그것은/을/를..."

---

## get — 4가지 용법

### 1. 움직임: Get up / Get over / Get out
### 2. 획득하다: I got a job.
### 3. ~되다 (get + 형용사): Did you get angry?
### 4. ~하게끔 하다 (get + 목적어 + pp): I got my car repaired.

---

## 감각동사 패턴 (look, sound, smell, taste, feel)

### Pattern 1 — 감각동사 + 형용사
* `It looked really serious.`

### Pattern 2 — 감각동사 + like + 명사
* `This looks like real leather.`

### Pattern 3 — 감각동사 + like + 완성된 문장
* `It looks like it's going to rain.`

---

## 비례식 표현

**구조:** the + 비교급 ~, the + 비교급 ~

| 한국어 | 영어 |
|--------|------|
| 많을수록 좋아 | The more, the better. |
| 이를수록 좋아 | The earlier, the better. |
| 작을수록 가격이 더 싸 | The smaller it is, the cheaper it is. |

---

## 완료시제 (have + p.p.) — 4가지 용법

| 용법 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| 경험 | ~한 적이 있다 | Have you ever been to New York? |
| 계속 | ~해왔다 | I've lived here since 2010. |
| 완료 | ~했다 (방금) | I've already seen the movie. |
| 결과 | ~해버렸다 | She has left. (지금 없다) |

---

## 조동사 완료시제 (추측)

| 표현 | 의미 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| must have + p.p. | ~했나봐 (강한 추측) | She must've had some problems. |
| might have + p.p. | ~했을 수도 있다 | She might have had health problems. |
| couldn't have + p.p. | ~했을 리가 없다 | It couldn't have been because she was sick. |
| should have + p.p. | ~했어야 했는데 (후회) | I should have studied harder. |

---

## 가정법 — 이럴 줄 알았으면

**구조:** If I had known ~, I would have / wouldn't have + p.p.

| 한국어 | 영어 |
|--------|------|
| 지루한 줄 알았더라면 안 했을 텐데 | If I had known it's boring, I wouldn't have started it. |
| 먼저 전화했더라면 시간 낭비 안 했을 텐데 | If I had called first, I wouldn't have wasted so much time. |
